Complete Life Story

Albert Einstein (1879–1955) was a German-born theoretical physicist who changed science forever. His theories reshaped our understanding of space, time, and energy.

Timeline of Einstein's Life

  • 1879: Born in Ulm, Germany
  • 1905: Releases 4 revolutionary papers
  • 1915: Completes General Relativity
  • 1921: Wins Nobel Prize
  • 1933: Moves to the U.S.
  • 1955: Dies in Princeton

Major Discoveries

  • Special Theory of Relativity
  • General Relativity
  • E = mc²
  • Photoelectric Effect
  • Bose–Einstein Condensate

Awards

  • Nobel Prize in Physics
  • Copley Medal
  • Royal Astronomical Society Gold Medal

Fun Facts

  • He rarely wore socks.
  • He loved the violin.
  • He was offered the presidency of Israel.
